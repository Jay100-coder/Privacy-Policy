Privacy Policy

Last updated: 02/22/2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data during testing. By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following data when you use our app for authentication and testing:

  • Pi Network Account Data: Includes your Pi Network account information (e.g., account ID, username) when you log in via Pi SDK.
  • Payment Data: If you make any in-game purchases or request payments, we collect transaction details necessary to process payments.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the data you provide for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate users through Pi Network.
  • To test Pi Network's payment functionality (for in-game purchases or transfers).
  • To troubleshoot and improve the user experience.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies or other tracking technologies to improve your experience during testing. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you share with us, but please note that no method of data transmission is 100% secure.
